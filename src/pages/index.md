# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Building Intelligent Humanoid Robots for the Future

This comprehensive textbook provides a complete educational journey into the world of physical AI and humanoid robotics. Through four progressive modules, students will learn to build intelligent systems that can perceive, reason, and act in the physical world.

### What You'll Learn

- **ROS 2 Fundamentals**: Master the Robot Operating System for robotics development
- **Digital Twin Technology**: Create virtual replicas for simulation and testing
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: Leverage advanced AI and simulation platforms
- **Vision-Language-Action Systems**: Build robots that understand and respond to natural language commands

### Who This Book Is For

This textbook is designed for computer science and engineering students, researchers, and professionals interested in robotics, artificial intelligence, and autonomous systems. No prior robotics experience is required, but basic programming knowledge is recommended.

### Educational Approach

Our curriculum follows constitutional pedagogical principles with:

- **Dual-track theory-practice design**: Each concept is immediately followed by hands-on exercises
- **Active learning exercises**: Interactive challenges every 2000 words
- **Worked examples**: Progressive understanding from black-box to glass-box
- **Tiered assessments**: Multiple difficulty levels (Tier 1-3) with rubrics
- **Accessibility first**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant content with alt-text for all diagrams
- **Interdisciplinary connections**: Integration across robotics, AI, and computer science

### Modules Overview

1. **Module 1**: ROS 2 fundamentals and navigation systems
2. **Module 2**: Digital twin creation and simulation environments
3. **Module 3**: NVIDIA Isaac platform integration and advanced simulation
4. **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action systems with voice command processing
