## How to Contribute

The "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics" textbook is an open educational resource. We welcome contributions from the community including:

- **Content Updates**: New examples, exercises, or explanations
- **Technical Corrections**: Bug fixes or accuracy improvements
- **Accessibility Enhancements**: Improved alt-text or navigation
- **Translation Support**: Making the content available in multiple languages
- **Educational Feedback**: Classroom testing results and pedagogical suggestions

To contribute, please visit our GitHub repository or contact the editorial team.
