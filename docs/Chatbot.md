# AI Assistant

Welcome to the interactive AI Assistant for the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook! This AI-powered assistant is designed to help you understand complex concepts, answer questions, and provide explanations based on the textbook content.

## How to Use

### Normal Chat
- Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of any page
- Type your question in the input box
- Press Enter or click the send button
- The AI will provide an answer with relevant sources

### Selected-Text Queries
- Highlight any text on the page
- Click the "Ask about this" button that appears above your selection
- The AI will provide an explanation of the selected text with additional context

## Features

- **Context-Aware Responses**: The AI understands the textbook content and provides accurate answers
- **Source Citations**: All responses include links to relevant textbook sections
- **Real-Time Interaction**: Get immediate answers to your questions
- **Powered by Cohere**: Advanced AI models for natural language understanding
- **Integrated with Qdrant**: Fast and accurate retrieval of relevant content

## Privacy & Accuracy

- Your conversations are not stored or logged
- Responses are generated based on textbook content only
- The AI follows strict accuracy guidelines to prevent hallucinations
- All answers are sourced from the textbook content

## Technical Details

This AI assistant uses:

- **Cohere**: For natural language understanding and generation
- **Qdrant**: For vector storage and retrieval of textbook content
- **Docusaurus**: For seamless integration with the textbook website
- **React**: For the interactive frontend components

The assistant retrieves relevant textbook sections and generates contextual responses based on the content, ensuring accuracy and relevance to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics curriculum.
